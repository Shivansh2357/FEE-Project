Our Services


Artist Profiles:

Artists can create their profiles, providing information about themselves, their artistic background, and their preferred art medium. They can also add their social media handles and contact details for potential buyers

Artwork Upload:

Artists can easily upload and manage their artwork by providing relevant details such as title, description, medium, dimensions, and pricing. They can also add multiple images of each artwork from different angles to give a better understanding of the piece.

Artwork Search and Filters:

Art enthusiasts can search for artwork based on different criteria such as artist name, art medium, price range, and art style. Filters can be implemented to enhance the search experience and help users find their desired artwork easily

Artwork Display and Promotion:

Online galleries showcase artists' work, providing a platform for them to display and promote their creations to a global audience.

Virtual Exhibitions:

Hosting virtual exhibitions or curated collections, allowing artists to showcase their work in a more curated and thematic manner.

Artwork Licensing:

Some platforms may offer licensing options for digital versions of artworks, allowing artists to monetize their creations in various ways

Art Advisory Services:

Some galleries provide art advisory services to help buyers and collectors make informed decisions about their purchases.
